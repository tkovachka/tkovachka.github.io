**Team Up** is a Flutter application that helps players find teammates for various sports events. Whether you're organizing a football match, want to join on a basketball game, or just want to get together and play pool, **Team Up** connects you with other players in your area.

## Overview

The idea for TeamUp was originally developed with two colleagues and friends of mine, Loren and Vladimir, during our time at university. It started as a simple project for a class, but then (with both their green light) I decided to **continue developing it for my graduation thesis.**

This is when the real work began, as I had to come up with a way to make the app more engaging and user-friendly, and additionally develop a whole **recommendation system** as the main functionality and focus of my thesis.

## AI in TeamUp

For me, the whole point of developing a recommendation system was to **incorporate AI into the project**. I quickly learnt that I don't even want to try to develop my own model by traditional machine learning methods - to waste time, gather enormous amounts of data, and train a model just for it to perform mediocre at best.

I decided to utilize what was already available and ready for me to take - **a big fat LLM**.

This project uses Gemini models with near perfect performance and results, and I managed to develop this LLM-driven recommendation system it **in just over a month**.

## Currently working on

TeamUp is very dear to me. It is the project that I graduated with, so it symbolizes **the end of an era**. In my eyes it would be a waste to let it collect dust on GitHub, so I am working on putting this app in **production**.

Currently working on hardening the recommendation system and limiting the API usage per user, so I can at least start testing this app with real users.

## Future Development

I see a lot of potential in this app. I have a list of features I want to implement, but for now I am determined on getting this app into the hands of **real users** and gathering some feedback.
